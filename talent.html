Talent pathway

From first touch to first contract.

Talent is universal; opportunity isn't. The GFE pathway is a verified, safeguarded route from grassroots football to the professional game — with no step depending on luck or connections.

Four stages

The route, step by step.

Discover

Registered grassroots coaches and credentialed scouts create verified player passports — confirmed age, baseline assessments and certified footage from real matches.

Develop

Players join partner academies or remote development programs. Progress is benchmarked each term against global peers of the same age and position.

Connect

Standout profiles surface to clubs through combines and network search. Trials are arranged with travel, guardianship and schooling safeguards handled.

Progress

Contracts complete with vetted representation and transparent terms. Development years convert into training compensation for every academy on the record.

Regional combines

Where the network takes the field.

Thirty-eight regional combines last season put verified prospects in front of credentialed scouts from hundreds of clubs — standardised testing, full matches, and every minute filmed and certified.

Combine format

Standardised testing

Sprint, agility, technical and cognitive batteries — identical at every combine, comparable across continents.

Combine format

Full-match play

Prospects play real 11-a-side football in balanced squads — because drills don't show game intelligence.

Combine format

Certified footage

Every minute is filmed, tagged and added to player passports — reviewable by any partner club within 48 hours.

Programs

Development that meets players where they are.

Ages 8–12

Grassroots Foundations

Coach education and equipment grants for community clubs — plus first player passports for standout kids, guardian-controlled from day one.

Ages 13–17

Academy Accelerator

Benchmarked curricula, dual-education requirements and welfare standards for partner academies — with combine slots for top cohorts.

Ages 18–21

Pro Bridge

Trial logistics, vetted representation and first-contract support for players stepping into the professional game — at home or abroad.

Women's game

Rising Pyramid

Dedicated combines, academy funding and pro-club matchmaking for the fastest-growing side of the ecosystem.

After playing

Second Half

Coaching badges, scouting credentials and sports-administration pathways that keep experience inside the game.

Safeguarding

Duty of Care

Age verification, guardian consent, capped agent fees and education guarantees — non-negotiable at every stage of the pathway.
