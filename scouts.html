Contact, the safeguarded way

Interest is welcome. Ambush isn't.

Step 1

Request through the passport

Every approach starts inside the platform — no direct messages to minors, ever. Your club identity and licence are attached automatically.

Step 2

Guardians & academy approve

The player's guardians and academy see the request, your history, and decide together. Every approach is logged on the safeguarding record.

Step 3

Introduction, on the record

Approved contact happens through the platform with guardians present. Development years stay attributed, so training compensation follows the player.
