The ecosystem

Every side of the game, one network.

Football only works when its parts work together. GFE gives each stakeholder purpose-built tools — and connects them through one verified, trusted layer.

01 · Players

Your career, in your hands.

Most players never control their own story. On GFE, your profile — footage, stats, medical record, development history — belongs to you, follows you between clubs and countries, and can't be rewritten by anyone else.

Identity

Verified player passport

One tamper-proof profile: confirmed age, playing history, positions, key metrics and certified match footage.

Visibility

Seen by the right people

Credentialed scouts and clubs across 94 countries can find you — with privacy controls set by you and your guardians.

Protection

Safeguarded moves

Every approach, trial and contract offer is logged and visible to your academy and family. No more disappearing middlemen.

02 · Clubs

Recruit like the biggest, whatever your budget.

Elite clubs run global scouting departments. GFE gives every professional club the same reach — verified talent search, structured trials and compliant transfer workflows.

Discovery

Global talent search

Filter 1.4M verified profiles by position, age band, metrics and development trajectory — not just highlight reels.

Workflow

Trial & shortlist management

Move targets through scouting, footage review, trial invitations and offers in one auditable pipeline.

Compliance

Clean transfers

Standardised documentation, minor-protection checks and automatic training-compensation calculations built in.

03 · Academies

Get paid for the players you build.

Academies do football's hardest work and too often see none of the reward. GFE records every development year — so when your graduate signs professionally, compensation flows back to you.

Development

Benchmarked curriculum

Track each cohort against anonymised global peers and adapt training plans with evidence, not guesswork.

Showcase

A global shop window

Publish graduate showcases to every partner club in the network and host credentialed scouting visits.

Revenue

Training compensation, tracked

Development years are registered on the player passport — $9.2M returned to network academies last season.

04 · Scouts

Your eye, on the record.

Scouting runs on trust and memory — and both leak value. GFE credentials scouts, structures their reports and attributes discoveries, so the first person to spot a talent shares in the outcome.

Credentials

Verified scout identity

Accreditation, track record and specialisms in one profile clubs can actually check.

Tools

Structured match reporting

Standardised templates, video tagging and offline capture for grounds without connectivity.

Attribution

First-flag rewards

Timestamped discovery records mean finder's fees are enforced by the network, not by goodwill.

05 · Agents & intermediaries

Professional representation, made visible.

Good agents change careers; bad ones end them. GFE separates the two with licence verification, transparent mandates and standardised agreements every family can understand.

Verification

Licence-checked profiles

Only verified, licensed intermediaries can approach players through the network.

Transparency

Open mandate registry

Who represents whom, since when, on what terms — visible to players, clubs and federations.

Standards

Fair-terms agreements

Plain-language representation contracts with capped fees for minors, in the family's own language.

06 · Fans & community

Back the journey, not just the jersey.

Fans fund football — GFE lets them fund its future. Follow emerging players from first trial to first cap, and put your support where it changes lives.

Follow

Journey tracking

Follow players and academies from grassroots up; get the story before the world knows the name.

Support

Scholarship funding

Sponsor boots, travel and school fees for academy players — with verified, itemised impact reports.

Belong

Grassroots club backing

Micro-patronage for local clubs: pitch maintenance, kit and coaching hours, funded by the crowd.
