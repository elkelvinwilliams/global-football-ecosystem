# GFE Founding Academy Program
### Your players, seen by the world — safely.

**For academies and grassroots clubs in Ghana, Senegal and Nigeria.**

## What your academy gets (free for founding partners)

- **A verified club passport** — licence badge, facilities, coaches, alumni
  and your playing style on film, visible to verified clubs and scouts
  worldwide
- **Player passports for your squad** — verified identity, stats and
  certified match video your players own for life
- **Training compensation, on the record** — every development year is
  registered; when your graduate signs professionally, your contribution is
  provable and attributable
- **Direct demand** — club requests and trial opportunities matched to your
  players, with your academy in the loop on every approach
- **Zero cost** — founding partners pay nothing; we onboard you hands-on,
  including filming support for your style film

## What we ask of you

1. **Verification** — registration documents and a named safeguarding
   officer (we help you formalise one if needed)
2. **Truth in data** — confirm only stats and fixtures you witnessed;
   provenance is the whole product
3. **Guardian engagement** — help us connect players' guardians; nothing
   involving a minor happens without them
4. **Feedback** — you're a founding partner: your workflow shapes the
   product

## How safeguarding works (the short version)

No agent, club or scout can contact your minor players directly — ever.
Approaches route to guardians and your academy together, every contact is
logged, mandates for minors require guardian signature with fee caps
flagged, and cross-border trials carry education guarantees. You can audit
everything.

## The founding cohort

25 academies across three markets. Founding partners get permanent
recognition on the platform, priority combine slots for top cohorts, and a
seat in our academy council.
