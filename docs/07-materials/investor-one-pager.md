# GFE — Global Football Ecosystem
### The trusted operating system of world football

**One line:** Verified football identity + safeguarded deal rails for the
sport's unseen majority — starting with elite African talent.

---

## The problem

Football's talent pipeline runs on trust that doesn't exist. Fake agents,
falsified ages, invisible mandates, unpaid training compensation and unsafe
approaches to minors are endemic — worst exactly where talent density is
highest. Clubs can't trust what they see; families can't trust who calls;
academies build stars and capture nothing.

## The product

A role-based platform where every participant is verified and every claim
carries provenance:

- **Player passports & football CVs** — verified identity, stats with
  provenance tiers, certified video, exportable and player-owned
- **Opportunity marketplace** — structured club requests, trials, mandates,
  sponsorships; AI-matched with explanations
- **Deal rails** — mandate registry with dual/guardian consent ceremonies,
  deal rooms, audit trails, records hash-anchored on-chain (zero PII)
- **Safeguarding as architecture** — no adult can initiate contact with a
  minor; every approach logged; guardian consent gates by design

## Why now

- FIFA's 2025 Global Transfer Report tracks **trials** formally for the
  first time — the industry is professionalising exactly the workflows we
  productise
- FIFA's agent-licensing regime makes agent verification tractable
- African federations and clubs are digitising with no incumbent trust layer

## Business model

Talent never pays to be seen. Demand side pays: club/scout subscriptions,
verification services, featured listings → sponsorship take-rate, success
fees, deal tooling, data/API products (Phase 2+). Target blended gross
margin >75%; infra <$0.15/MAU.

## Go-to-market

Concierge wedge: ~25 hand-onboarded academies across Ghana, Senegal,
Nigeria; their best 500 players verified; matched to 20 trusted club/scout
partners. Every verified trial becomes a public case study. Expansion:
Africa → Europe demand → Middle East → Asia/NA.

## Moat

The verified identity graph, the safeguarding track record, the
provenance-graded dataset (including labelled football video), and anchored
trust records with real switching costs.
