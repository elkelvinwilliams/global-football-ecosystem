Clubs & academies

Your football. On the record.

Every club and academy in the network carries its own passport — playing style on film, development record, facilities and compliance, all verified. Four illustrative club passports below, all demo data.

Playing style, on film

Every club passport starts with a film.

Step 1

Film the prompts

A guided template — eight prompts covering build-up, pressing, transitions and set pieces. Film your own sessions and matches; any modern phone will do.

Step 2

Vision certifies it

GFE Vision checks the footage is really yours, really recent, and cuts it against your match data — so the film shows how you actually play, not how you'd like to.

Step 3

It lives on your passport

The style film sits on your club passport beside your record. Scouts and parents see your football before they see your results.
