Every stakeholder in football gets a home in GFE — with tools built for how they actually work, and a network that connects them to everyone else.

How it works

From first touch to first contract.

One connected pathway takes a player from a village pitch to a professional dressing room — with every step verified along the way.

Discover

Grassroots coaches and credentialed scouts register talent with verified footage and standardised assessments.

Develop

Partner academies benchmark each player's growth against global peers and share development plans across the network.

Connect

Clubs search the network, request footage and arrange trials — with the player's academy and family in the loop at every step.

Progress

Signings complete with transparent, compliant paperwork — and training compensation flows back to the academies that built the player.
